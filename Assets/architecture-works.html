Architecture Works

All rendering and graphic works here are produced throughout my architecture journey, from university up to my latest architecture firm experience.

*Selected Works

My architecture renderings consists of photorealistic renderings and post-digital renderings. Photorealistic renderings, as the name suggests, are renderings made to look as close to real life as possible whereas post-digital renderings are a bit more abstract and surreal.

All renderings are modeled in Sketchup and rendered with V-Ray. Other graphic works are done in Photoshop, Illustrator and AutoCAD.
