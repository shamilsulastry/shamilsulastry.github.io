*UI / UX

YANG KECIL

Yang Kecil makes comfortable apparel for toddlers from 6 to 18 months old. Their collection includes rompers and shirts with cute little animal and floral patterns.

I set out to design a website that is easy to navigate, vibrant and fun to reflect the playful nature of the brand.

*The Challenge

The Yang Kecil website is a self-directed project and my introduction to Figma as a design and prototyping tool. I wanted to capture the colorful and fun essence of the pre-existing logo and translate it into a website. I also did some product photography for the brand.

*Wireframe

The first step of my design process was sketching out some wireframe ideas and producing a high fidelity version in Figma. A mapped out the landing page, product catalogue page and product page with the necessary elements and put it into a layout.

Landing Page - The landing page needs to succinctly convey what the page is selling and a clear call to action.

With this in mind, the first thing that the users see upon arriving at the landing page is a short copy with the logo, a “Shop” call to action and the latest collection of clothes available.

Product Catalogue - The product catalogue page is organized at 3 products per row with the most important information (Name, Price and a Buy button).

Product Page - The product page displays the full info for each product with recommendation of similar products

*Final Design

All of the UI elements and colors are derived from the logo.

RUBIK is used as the main typeface because of its clear sans serif font family. The slightly rounded corners also reflects the fun nature of the website whilst retaining a clear sense of legibility.
